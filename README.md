# Circana (circana)
Circana (formerly IRI and The NPD Group) is the leading advisor on the complexity of consumer behavior, providing data-driven insights, analytics, and technology solutions that help almost 7,000 brands and retailers understand and predict consumer purchasing patterns across retail, CPG, beauty, foodservice, healthcare, and technology sectors covering $4T+ in global consumer spending across 26 industries.
